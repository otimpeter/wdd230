Our Purpose

Our purpose is three fold. First, to increase consumer knowledge about nutritious, high quality, shelf stable foods by providing free monthly cooking classes featuring food storage items. We are firm believers in, "Eating what you store and storing what you eat!". Secondly, to support American farmers and manufacturers whenever possible. And thirdly, to provide premium quality products at affordable prices so our customers can stretch their food dollars and be better prepared to weather unforeseen hardships.

Our Food and Funding

Between food drives and direct donations, individuals contribute more than 30% of the food we distribute through our market, Pantry Packs aimed at alleviating child hunger over the weekends, and partner organizations. Through our Grocery Rescue partners we saved 851,605 pounds of perishable food last year that would have otherwise gone to waste. Local farms and gardeners donate excess fresh produce throughout the harvest months. Utah Food Bank provides produce and USDA food. With these food donations along with milk purchased at a discount, we are able to provide a broad spectrum of foods enough for approximately 1.7 million meals.

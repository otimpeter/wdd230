We advocate for the creation of a favorable commercial, trade and investment environment that supports enterprise expansion. The membership of Otim chamber of Commerce constitutes of small, micro enterprises (MSEs), medium and large enterprises mostly in the city of kampala Uganda.

Become A Member

To become a OCM member, one is required to Be of 18 years and above; Be a company registered by the Registrar of Companies; Have a business premise to address Membership Fees; pay a one-time registration fee of 10,000 Uganda Shillings; and pay subscription fees in one of the following categories Ordinary, Silver, Gold and Associate levels.

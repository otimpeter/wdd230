Become A Member

To become a Otim Chamber of Commerce member, one is required to Be of 18 years and above; Be a company registered by the Registrar of Companies; Have a business premise to address Membership Fees; pay a one-time registration fee of 10,000 Uganda Shillings; and pay subscription fees in one of the following categories Ordinary, Silver, Gold and Associate levels
